Discovery: 2005, by Mike Brown and team

Type: Dwarf Planet

Size: ~2,326 km diameter

Distance from Earth: ~14.5 billion km (varies)

Description: Eris is one of the largest dwarf planets in the Kuiper Belt. It is slightly smaller than Pluto but more massive.

Fun Fact: Its discovery led to Pluto being reclassified as a dwarf planet.
