Europa AI Assistant

Discovery: 1610, by Galileo Galilei

Parent Planet: Jupiter

Size: ~3,122 km diameter

Distance from Earth: ~628 million km

Description: Europa is one of Jupiter’s largest moons. It has a smooth icy surface and likely hides a global subsurface ocean beneath its crust.

Fun Fact: Europa is one of the top candidates for extraterrestrial life in our solar system.

Breathing / Oxygen

Thin oxygen atmosphere exists, but far too tenuous for humans.

Water & Sewage

Water ice is abundant — can be melted and recycled for use.

Food Production

Greenhouses and hydroponic systems needed for colonies.

Energy

Solar power is limited. Nuclear reactors are more reliable.

Shelter

Radiation from Jupiter requires underground or shielded habitats.
