Moon AI Assistant

Discovery: Known since prehistoric times

Parent Planet: Earth

Size: ~3,474 km diameter

Distance from Earth: ~384,400 km

Description: The Moon is Earth’s only natural satellite. It influences tides, stabilizes Earth’s rotation, and has been the site of human exploration since Apollo missions.

Fun Fact: The Moon is slowly drifting away from Earth at about 3.8 cm per year.

Breathing / Oxygen

No breathable atmosphere. Oxygen must be generated from lunar regolith or brought from Earth.

Water & Sewage

Water ice exists in permanently shadowed craters near the poles. Recycling systems are still vital for colonies.

Food Production

Food requires hydroponic or soil-based greenhouses inside sealed habitats.

Energy

Solar power is strong and reliable, especially on the lunar surface. Nuclear power could provide backup energy.

Shelter

Bases must protect against radiation, micrometeorites, and temperature extremes. Lava tubes may serve as natural shelters.
