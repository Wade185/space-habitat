Mercury AI Assistant

Discovery: Known since ancient times

Parent Star: Sun

Size: ~4,879 km diameter (smallest planet)

Distance from Earth: ~77 million km (closest approach)

Description: Mercury is the smallest planet in the Solar System and the closest to the Sun. It has extreme temperature swings, almost no atmosphere, and a heavily cratered surface.

Fun Fact: A day on Mercury (sunrise to sunrise) lasts 176 Earth days!

Breathing / Oxygen

Mercury has no breathable atmosphere. Life-support systems are required for humans.

Water & Sewage

Water ice exists in permanently shadowed craters near Mercury’s poles, but must be mined and purified. Recycling systems would handle sewage.

Food Production

Food can only be grown in sealed greenhouses using hydroponics or aeroponics.

Energy

Solar power is extremely strong on Mercury due to its closeness to the Sun, making it the most practical energy source.

Shelter

Habitats must be built underground or with thick shielding to survive extreme temperature swings (from -173°C at night to +427°C in the day).
