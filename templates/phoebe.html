Phoebe AI Assistant

Discovery: 1899 by William Henry Pickering

Parent Planet: Saturn

Size: ~213 km diameter

Distance from Earth: ~1.3 billion km

Description: Phoebe is a retrograde irregular moon of Saturn, thought to be a captured Kuiper Belt object.

Fun Fact: Unlike most Saturnian moons, Phoebe is very dark, reflecting only about 6% of sunlight.

Breathing / Oxygen

Phoebe has no atmosphere. Colonists would depend entirely on sealed habitats.

Water & Sewage

Traces of water ice exist but extraction is difficult. Imports would be needed from icy moons.

Food Production

No native sources. Hydroponics and greenhouses required for colonists.

Energy

Too far for effective solar. Nuclear power is the practical choice.

Shelter

Colonies must be heavily shielded from radiation and impacts.
