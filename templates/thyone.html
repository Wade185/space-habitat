Thyone AI Assistant

Discovery: 2001 by Scott S. Sheppard

Parent Planet: Jupiter

Size: ~4 km diameter

Distance from Earth: ~628 million km

Description: Thyone is one of Jupiter’s small irregular moons, part of the Ananke group, with a retrograde orbit.

Fun Fact: Like its group members, Thyone likely originated as a captured asteroid.

Breathing / Oxygen

Thyone has no atmosphere. Oxygen would need to be generated through electrolysis of imported water or produced in closed-loop life support systems with plants and algae.

Water & Sewage

No native water sources exist. Colonists must import ice from other Jovian moons like Europa or recycle all water in closed systems.

Food Production

Hydroponics and synthetic food production would be required in sealed habitats.

Energy

Nuclear power is the most reliable. Solar energy is weak at Jupiter’s distance and requires large arrays.

Shelter

Radiation from Jupiter is intense. Habitats must be underground or shielded with regolith to protect life.
