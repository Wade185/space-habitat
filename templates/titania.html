Titania AI Assistant

Discovery: 1787 by William Herschel

Parent Planet: Uranus

Size: ~1580 km diameter

Distance from Earth: ~2.9 billion km

Description: Titania is the largest moon of Uranus, with a heavily cratered surface and icy composition.

Fun Fact: Titania has large faults and canyons up to 20 km deep.

Breathing / Oxygen

No atmosphere. Oxygen must be provided in sealed habitats.

Water & Sewage

No surface water. Ice must be mined or imported and recycled.

Food Production

Hydroponics or lab-grown food is required for colonies.

Energy

Solar is weak; nuclear reactors are preferred.

Shelter

Pressurized habitats or underground shelters with radiation protection required.
