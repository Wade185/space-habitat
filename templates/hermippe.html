Description: Hermippe is one of Jupiter’s small outer irregular moons, part of the Ananke group.

Fun Fact: Hermippe’s orbit is retrograde, meaning it moves opposite to Jupiter’s rotation.

  • Plants inside greenhouses converting CO₂ into O₂.
  • Artificial oxygen generators splitting CO₂.
  • Storage tanks for emergencies.

Shelter
