Breathing / Oxygen

Water & Sewage

No liquid water on Uranus’ surface. Water must come from ice or chemical extraction.

Food Production

Hydroponics or lab-grown food would be mandatory in orbital or floating colonies.

Energy

Solar energy is weak at Uranus’ distance; nuclear reactors are preferable.

Shelter

Colonies would need to float in the atmosphere or be in orbital habitats with radiation shielding.
