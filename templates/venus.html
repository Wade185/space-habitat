Venus AI Assistant

Discovery: Known since ancient times

Type: Terrestrial Planet

Diameter: ~12,104 km

Distance from Earth: ~41 million to 261 million km (varies)

Description: Venus is the second planet from the Sun with a thick, toxic atmosphere mostly of CO₂ and surface temperatures around 465°C.

Fun Fact: Venus rotates retrograde, meaning it spins in the opposite direction to most planets.

Breathing / Oxygen

Atmosphere is mostly CO₂ and sulfuric acid clouds. Breathing requires full life-support systems.

Water & Sewage

No liquid water on the surface. Water must be produced in colonies using imported or recycled sources.

Food Production

Hydroponics and lab-grown food inside pressurized domes are mandatory.

Energy

Solar energy is strong, but extreme temperatures and acid clouds require robust shielding.

Shelter

Colonies must be in high-altitude floating habitats or reinforced surface domes with protective coatings.
