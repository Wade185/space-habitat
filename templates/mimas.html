Parent Planet: Saturn

Size: ~396 km diameter

Distance from Earth: ~1.3 billion km

Description: Mimas is famous for its huge Herschel Crater, giving it the look of the “Death Star.” Its icy surface shows signs of impact history.

Fun Fact: Despite its size, Mimas may hide a liquid water ocean beneath its crust.
