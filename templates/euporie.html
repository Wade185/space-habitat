Europie AI Assistant

Discovery: 2001, by Scott S. Sheppard and team

Parent Planet: Jupiter

Size: ~3 km diameter

Distance from Earth: ~600 million km (varies)

Description: Europie is a small retrograde irregular moon of Jupiter, part of the Ananke group of captured bodies.

Fun Fact: Europie’s orbit is tilted and retrograde, meaning it moves opposite to Jupiter’s rotation.

Breathing / Oxygen

No atmosphere. Oxygen must be supplied artificially.

Water & Sewage

No natural water. Recycling and imports are mandatory.

Food Production

Farming is only possible in sealed hydroponic domes.

Energy

Solar is too weak. Nuclear energy is needed.

Shelter

Colonies must be shielded from radiation and impacts.
